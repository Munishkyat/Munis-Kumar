FRUITS





Apple trees need at least 8 hours of sun per day during the
growing season. Two varieties are required for successful
pollination; one can be a crabapple. Dwarf apple trees
will start bearing fruit 2 to 3 years after planting.
Standard size trees can take up to 8 years to bear fruit.
Some varieties are more susceptible to insect and disease
damage than others. Prune annually to keep apple trees
healthy and productive.
